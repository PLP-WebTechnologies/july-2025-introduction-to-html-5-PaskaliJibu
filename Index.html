Why Child Nutrition Matters

Proper nutrition during childhood is critical for healthy growth, brain development, and disease prevention. Poor nutrition can lead to stunting, wasting, and long-term health issues.

Our Nutrition Services

Growth Monitoring

We provide regular height and weight checks to monitor child development and detect malnutrition early.

Dietary Counseling

Our experts offer personalized nutrition advice to parents and caregivers based on age, health status, and food availability.

Supplementation Programs

We support vitamin A distribution, deworming, and iron supplementation to address micronutrient deficiencies.

Resources for Parents and Caregivers

We provide educational materials on breastfeeding, complementary feeding, hygiene, and the six food groups essential for children's nutrition.
